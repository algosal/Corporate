# 🧩 ENTITY ACTIVATION CHECKLIST (IP → ACTIVE COMPANY)

## 🟣 STAGE 0 — IP LAYER (Default State)

Applies to:

- Savvy Inventory
- any future concept companies (like Mellow Enclave)

### Status definition:

Idea, architecture, or prototype only. No financial or legal activity.

---

# 🚪 STAGE 1 — ACTIVATION TRIGGERS (must meet ALL)

A company moves from **IP → Active** ONLY if ALL conditions below are true:

## 1. Real Use Case Exists

- ✔ There is a real market need (not theoretical)
- ✔ At least 1 clear customer segment identified
- ✔ Problem is already being solved poorly in the market

---

## 2. Product is Technically Deployable

- ✔ MVP exists OR is buildable within 30–90 days
- ✔ Core architecture is defined (not just idea-level)
- ✔ No dependency on “unknown external approval” (e.g. APIs not confirmed = NOT ready)

---

## 3. Revenue Path is Defined

- ✔ Clear monetization model exists:
  - SaaS subscription OR licensing OR service fee

- ✔ Pricing structure is defined
- ✔ You can explain how money comes in within 10 seconds

---

## 4. Operational Commitment Exists

- ✔ You or a team are actively building it weekly
- ✔ It has execution priority (not “side idea”)
- ✔ Time allocation is real (not hypothetical)

---

## 5. Legal Structure Justified

- ✔ Separate entity is actually needed for:
  - liability
  - contracts
  - clients

- ❌ NOT just for organizational preference

---

# 🟢 STAGE 2 — ACTIVATION (IP → ACTIVE)

Once all triggers are met:

### You are allowed to:

- Open business bank account
- Start revenue collection
- Sign contracts under entity name
- Move it under holding structure (future FinSln layer)
- Track formal expenses and revenue

---

# 🔵 STAGE 3 — ACTIVE OPERATION RULES

Applies once entity is activated.

## Mandatory rules:

### ✔ Financial separation

- No mixing with other entities unless documented intercompany agreement exists

### ✔ Bank activity required

- Must show real inflow/outflow (no dormant accounts)

### ✔ Reporting consistency

- Revenue must align with business purpose
- No “random transfers”

---

# 🟡 STAGE 4 — HOLDING STRUCTURE ELIGIBILITY

An active company can ONLY be moved under FinSln Holdings if:

## Required conditions:

- ✔ Stable revenue (3–6 months minimum)
- ✔ Predictable cash flow
- ✔ Clean accounting history
- ✔ Defined ownership structure

---

# 🚫 HARD REJECTION CONDITIONS (DO NOT ACTIVATE IF…)

A company MUST remain IP if:

- ❌ API / core dependency is not available (your Savvy Inventory case right now)
- ❌ No customer demand proven
- ❌ No MVP exists or is not realistic to build soon
- ❌ It exists only as “future vision”
- ❌ No time is allocated to build it
